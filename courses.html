Admission Process

  1. Fill the Online Admission Form.
  2. Upload Required Documents.
  3. Pay the Registration Fee.
  4. Attend Certificate Verification.
  5. Receive Admission Confirmation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed Intermediate (10+2) with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry for B.Tech.
  • Graduation in any discipline for MBA.
  • Valid entrance examination score (if applicable).
  • Original educational certificates must be submitted during admission.
